The Concept

Where Your
Choices
Tell the Story

The Brew Lab puts you behind the counter as the barista on duty. Every decision you make determines whether the preparation succeeds — or fails spectacularly. Process matters. This experience proves it.

01

Choose a Menu Item

Coffee, Yoghurt Bowl, or Matcha. Each carries its own preparation logic and branching path of decisions.

02

Watch & Decide

Each stage plays a short video. Watch carefully — the details of the preparation are your only clues. Decisions appear directly over the footage.

03

Face the Outcome

Make the right calls and reach a perfect result. Make the wrong call and the preparation ends immediately. The quality is yours to own.
