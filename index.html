How It Works

A quick guide before you step behind the counter

1

Choose a Menu Item

Pick one of three items from The Brew Lab menu — Coffee, Yoghurt Bowl, or Matcha. Each carries its own preparation logic.

↓
2

Watch the Stage Video

Each stage plays a short video showing part of the preparation process. Watch carefully — the details in the video are your only clues.

↓
3

Make Your Decision

At the end of each stage you'll face a critical two-option choice. Only one is correct. Choose wrong and the preparation ends immediately.

↓
4

Reach the Outcome

Nail both decisions and you'll complete the preparation with a perfect result. The core message: process matters.
